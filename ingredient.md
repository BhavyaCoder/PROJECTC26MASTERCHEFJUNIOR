
                       Pinata Cake  

INGREDIENTS:
For Chocolate Cake:
* Oreo Biscuit - 1 packet 
* Milk - less than 1/2 cup 
* Sugar Powder - 2 spoon 
* Baking Powder - 1/4 tsp 
* Oil - for greasing

For Pinata Cake:
* White Chocolate - 300 gram 
* Silicon Mold

For Decoration:
* Kiwi 
* Cherries
* Melted White Chocolate 
* Edable Stars 
* Silver Spray
* Dark Chocolate
